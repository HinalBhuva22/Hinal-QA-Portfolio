### Professional Approach

While delivering a smooth and intuitive user experience is important, I also focus on the business logic, system behavior, data integrity, and workflows that power the product behind the scenes. Some of the most critical defects are not always visible to users, yet they can significantly impact business operations, customer trust, and overall product success.

My approach is to evaluate software from both a user and business perspective, ensuring that features not only work as expected but also support the intended business goals. I focus on identifying risks, validating critical workflows, and improving product reliability to help teams deliver stable, high-quality releases with confidence.

### Testing Philosophy

Quality is not just about finding defects, it's about building confidence in the product. I believe effective testing requires a deep understanding of business goals, user journeys, and critical workflows to ensure software performs reliably under real-world conditions.

My testing approach focuses on proactive risk identification, thorough validation, and continuous improvement. I also believe that collaboration is key to delivering high-quality software. By working closely with developers, product managers, designers, and stakeholders, I help ensure clear communication, faster issue resolution, and successful product releases. Through a combination of teamwork, quality-focused thinking, and attention to detail, I strive to deliver software that meets both user expectations and business objectives.
